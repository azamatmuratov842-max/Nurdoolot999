Present Simple Tense

The Present Simple is used to describe habits, routines, and general truths. We use it for things that happen regularly.

📖 GRAMMAR RULE
Subject + Verb (+ s/es for he/she/it)
I work every day.  |  She works at a school.
🎯 Quiz: Choose the correct answer
She _____ to school every day.
